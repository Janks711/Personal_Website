The Best Games According to Michael

My top 3 games of all time

Bloodborne

A mix of great combat, fantastic bosses and incredible level design. Set in gothic Victorian era setting makes this an unforgettable classic.

Zelda Ocarina of Time

The game that set a high level of Standard for open world games. The game that made me want to become a game developer. Fantastic game.

Metal Gear Solid

One of the first PlayStation games I ever played and the game that got me hooked to what is a fantastic espionage series.
